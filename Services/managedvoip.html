Managed VoIP FAQ

What is VoIP and how does it work?

VoIP replaces traditional phone lines with an internet-based system. Your calls travel as digital data packets over the internet, eliminating the need for physical phone lines. This allows for clearer call quality, more features, and significant cost savings.

Does our VoIP come with a digital receptionist?

Yes, our robust program allows for the system to greet callers, gather information using pre-recorded messages and provide seamless connection to the appropriate person or department

What internet speed is needed for VoIP?

A stable internet connection and upload speed is recommended for good VoIP call quality.

How secure are VoIP calls?

As your VoIP provider, we implement security measures to protect your calls and data.

How easy is setting and using VoIP?

Setting up VoIP with us is seamless and smooth. We offer user-friendly interfaces and readily available customer support to assist you.

Advantages of using our VoIP Services

Here's why our VoIP services are the best solution for your business communication needs:

Exceptional Customer Support

We believe in providing exceptional customer support to ensure you get the most out of our VoIP services. Our team of knowledgeable and friendly professionals is readily available to assist you with installation, configuration, and ongoing technical support. We offer a variety of resources, including tutorials, FAQs, and live support options, to empower you to utilize all the features and benefits of our VoIP system.

Flexible Options

Unlike traditional phone systems with limited lines, VoIP scales effortlessly to meet your growing needs. Adding new lines is a breeze, and you're not restricted by physical hardware limitations. Furthermore, VoIP allows your team members to use their preferred devices, from desktop computers and laptops to smartphones and tablets, to make and receive calls. This flexibility fosters a more mobile work environment and empowers your team to stay connected regardless of location.

Cost Savings

VoIP eliminates the need for traditional landlines and reduces long-distance call charges. With VoIP, all your calls travel over your existing internet connection, leading to significant cost savings, particularly for businesses that make frequent long-distance calls or have geographically dispersed teams. Additionally, VoIP offers packages that include features like voicemail, call forwarding, and conferencing at a fixed monthly rate, making budgeting easier.
