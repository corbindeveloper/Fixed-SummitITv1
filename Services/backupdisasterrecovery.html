Why you need Backup & Recovery

The common threats and errors that can happen

Hardware Failure

Hardware failure is the malfunctioning of a computer's physical components. This can range from minor inconveniences to complete system crashes. A failing hard drive, for example, might make strange noises or exhibit slow performance before completely losing stored data. Other common hardware failures include overheating processors, failing power supplies, and faulty RAM (random access memory), which can cause crashes or unexpected shutdowns.

Cyber Threats

In this growing industry, malicious hackers and scammers have become common. They may steal your data and force your business to pay a ransom. Their meddling may also corrupt your files in the process. Imagine the time and energy, how much your business would have to work to recreate these files.

With our data recovery plan, we give support. We combat them by having your data on the ready. You minimize data loss, as your stolen files will be ready, and you minimize the time significantly in gathering your files back.

Accidental Deletion

Sometimes the data loss may not even be because of the technology or a hacker, but a simple accident by a fellow co-worker. No one is perfect, but that is precisely why you want to ensure your business has a saftey net.

With Summit there is total relief knowing that we are on your side at the ready prepared for any accidents or hiccups that may happen to your business.
