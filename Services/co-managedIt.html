What are Co-Managed IT Services?

Experience peak collaboration with Summit

Co-Managed IT is a collaboration between your internal IT Team and Summit IT Solutions, together we empower your company's IT needs. In essence, co-managed IT creates a tag team approach to your IT management. Your internal team handles the daily tasks they know best, while Summit acts as your trusted IT advisor. We bring a toolbox overflowing with specialized knowledge and extra resources to tackle complex issues or fill any gaps. This lets you stay in the driver's seat with your IT operations, but with the calmness that comes from having seasoned IT professionals on standby.


You have complete control over which IT functions remain in-house and which benefit from Summit IT's expert oversight. This allows you to optimize resource allocation and leverage internal strengths. By strategically utilizing our expertise, you can avoid the significant expense of hiring and training a larger internal IT team. You only pay for the specific services your organization requires.
